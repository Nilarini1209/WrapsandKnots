Our Story

At Wraps and Knots, our journey began with a simple love for flowers and a deep belief in their power to express the unspoken. What started as a small dream has blossomed into a space where creativity meets emotions — where every bouquet is crafted with care, passion, and purpose. Whether it's a joyful celebration, a heartfelt apology, or a quiet moment of reflection, we are here to help you say it with flowers. Rooted in quality, elegance, and a personal touch, Wraps and Knots is more than just a florist — it's a story of connections wrapped in blooms and tied with meaning.
